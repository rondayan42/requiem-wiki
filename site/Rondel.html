Rondel

From Requiem Wiki

Background

After the Crescent Mine was closed, the scholar Rondel Hallons managed to find a rich mineral vein at the center of the island. This vein became known as Fridha Mine, and Rondel's family and the miners established Rondel Village. This village is still less than ten years old, and many parts of it are still under construction. The village used to be bustling with migrants until monsters infested the Fridha Mine, the source of the entire village's livelihood. Although this has plunged many of the villagers into despair, there are some that still hold onto the hope that the Temperions will come to save their village.
