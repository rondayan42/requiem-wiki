Nova Lux

From Requiem Wiki

Background

The Xenon Order suffered a fall in prestige and influence since the fall of the Zudekha, and began to construct a huge village in Parness in hopes of restoring its power. This village developed into the city of Nova Lux, the City of Light. Nova Lux is now governed by Lady Katrin Cordier, known around the world as the Woman Saint. Lady Cordier current faces the challenge of internal conflicts in Nova Lux while waging war against the dark Neubellon forces.
