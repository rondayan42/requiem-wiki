Crossbows

From Requiem Wiki

  • Crossbows have a base Speed of 3.0.
  • Crossbows stat bonuses are generally limited to DEX, CON, MND, HP, and HP Regen.
  • Crossbows are equipped in the Two Hands slot.
  • Reinforced Items are non compoundable and bound to character when equiped.
