Level Requirement: 50

Description

Protectors are excellent in protecting their parties, armed with combat skills and divine magic that will recover HP and offer protection from enemies for themselves and their party members.

Protectors have less attack power compared to Commanders, but they have better survivability due to self healing and they can use potent magic to assist their allies on the battlefield.
