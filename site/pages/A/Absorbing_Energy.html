Type: Offensive Skill

Description

Add magic on magic weapon for attacks. Some amount of HP recovers by chance.

  • Skill can Critical Heal. Skill Heal amount will double.
  • Additional Heal is affected by MND.
  • Additional Heal is affected by INT, ONLY when critical occurs.
  • Every 5INT = +1HP Heal.
  • Every 5MND = +1HP Heal. If critical occurs, amount is doubled.
  • Can be upgraded with DNA (max +25% success chance)
  • Damage based on character's Physical Attack Power instead of Magical Attack Power
