Crescent

From Requiem Wiki

Background

Crescent is a huge island located southeast of Parness that has long been abandoned due to its cold climate and barren soil. In 52 AT, a group of settlers led by a widow named Anela Valdes arrived on the island, discovered a rich mineral vein, and began to develop the island. The first twenty years they spent developing the island is known as Crescent's Golden Age. However, Crescent's fate drastically changed after Anela was tragically murdered by her son Edin. Most of the island has fallen under the control of the Ioxenics and the Guildenstein Merchant Guild ever since.
