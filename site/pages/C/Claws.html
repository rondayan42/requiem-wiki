Claws

From Requiem Wiki

  • Claws have a base Speed of 1.6.
  • Claws stat bonuses are generally limited to STR, DEX, CON, HP, and HP Regen.
  • Claws provide a set amount of Critical Chance bonus in addition to any randomly generated stats.
  • Claws are equipped in the Dual Wield slot.
  • Reinforced Items are non compoundable and bound to character when equiped.
