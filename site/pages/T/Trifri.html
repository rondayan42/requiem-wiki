Trifri

From Requiem Wiki

Background

Trifri Village was built where an ancient airship fell in the Plateau of South Hammerine Wood. That crashed airship still stands there, and is a common sight to the villages that graze their livestock on the nearby plateau. No one has dared to climb to the higher parts of the crashed airship, but many scholars believe that some remnants of the Zudekha civilization can be found there.
