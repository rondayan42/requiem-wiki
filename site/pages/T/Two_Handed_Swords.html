Two Handed Swords

From Requiem Wiki

  • Two Handed Swords have a base Speed of 2.0.
  • Two Handed Swords stat bonuses are generally limited to STR, DEX, CON, HP, and HP Regen.
  • Two Handed Swords provide a set amount of Evasion bonus in addition to any randomly generated stats.
  • Two Handed Swords are equipped in the Two Hands slot.
  • Reinforced Items are non compoundable and bound to character when equiped.
