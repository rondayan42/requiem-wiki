Two Handed Bludgeons

From Requiem Wiki

  • Two Handed Bludgeons have a base Speed of 2.4.
  • Two Handed Bludgeons stat bonuses are generally limited to STR, CON, MND, HP, and HP Regen.
  • Two Handed Bludgeons provide a set amount of Accuracy bonus in addition to any randomly generated stats.
  • Two Handed Bludgeons are equipped in the Two Hands slot.
  • Reinforced Items are non compoundable and bound to character when equiped.
