Temperion Training Camp

From Requiem Wiki

Description

This is the first area that players start in after character creation.

Notes

Once you leave this dungeon you cannot return. Be sure to pick up the The Very First Bit of Advice quest from NPC Harta at the dungeon exit before you leave.

As of the beginning of open beta, this dungeon was no longer accessable.
