Job Overview

The Tempest is an Electric Class Magician Specializing in Wide Range Offense

Characteristics

Tempests specialize in magic attacks that are more destructive than divine magic. They can cast wide range electric shock skills that can cause as much destruction as a typhoon, the Tempest's namesake.

Combat Style

Tempests equip Wands and Staffs and use support magic to strengthen their magical combat abilities. They can inflict serious damage to many enemies within the wide range of their attacks.
