Magic Shields

From Requiem Wiki

  • Magic Shields have a Block Chance of 30%.
  • Magic Shields stat bonuses are generally limited to INT, CON, MND, MP, and MP Regen.
  • Magic Shields have a guaranteed high INT bonus in addition to any randomly generated stats.
  • Magic Shields are equipped in the Left Hand slot.
