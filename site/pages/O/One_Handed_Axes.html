One Handed Axes

From Requiem Wiki

  • One Handed Axes have a base Speed of 1.6.
  • One Handed Axes stat bonuses are generally limited to DEX, CON, MND, HP, and HP Regen.
  • One Handed Axes provide a set amount of Critical Chance bonus in addition to any randomly generated stats.
  • One Handed Axes are equipped in the Right Hand slot.
  • Reinforced Items are non compoundable and bound to character when equiped.
