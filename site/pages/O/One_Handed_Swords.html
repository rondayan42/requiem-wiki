One Handed Swords

From Requiem Wiki

  • One Handed Swords have a base Speed of 1.5.
  • One Handed Swords stat bonuses are generally limited to STR, DEX, CON, HP, and HP Regen.
  • One Handed Swords provide a set amount of Evasion bonus in addition to any randomly generated stats.
  • One Handed Swords are equipped in the Right Hand slot.
  • Reinforced Items are non compoundable and bound to character when equiped.
