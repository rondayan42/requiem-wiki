Description

Warlords are trained to resist enemy provocation and support their allies on the battlefield. However, they are hampered by slow speed and weak long range skills.

Warlords use Two-Handed Weapons, but cannot inflict great damage like Warriors or Berserkers. Instead, they specialize in providing support.
