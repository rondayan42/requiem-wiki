Wands

From Requiem Wiki

  • Wands have a base Speed of 1.5.
  • Wands add to both physical and magical damage.
  • Wands stat bonuses are generally limited to INT, CON, MND, MP, and MP Regen.
  • Wands have a guaranteed high INT bonus in addition to any randomly generated stats.
  • Wands are equipped in the Right Hand slot.
  • Reinforced Items are non compoundable and bound to character when equiped.
