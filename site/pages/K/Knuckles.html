Knuckles

From Requiem Wiki

  • Knuckles have a base Speed of 1.7.
  • Knuckles stat bonuses are generally limited to STR, DEX, CON, HP and HP Regen.
  • Knuckles are equipped in the Dual Wield slot.
  • Reinforced Items are non compoundable and bound to character when equiped.
