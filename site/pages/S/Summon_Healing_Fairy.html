Description

Summon a Healing Spirit that continuously restores HP of Party Members within this skill's range for a while.

  • Effects up to 8 targets.

Note

  • This skill currently does not increase heal size with increased MND. It heals only for the amount listed on the skill tooltip.
  • HP restoration is doubled for other members of the party. For example: Summon Healing Fairy level 1 heals 2 x 116 immediately after being cast for party members other than the caster.
  • HP restoration lasts for 60 seconds
