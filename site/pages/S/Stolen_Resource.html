Stolen Resource
Requirements
Level: 49
Item(s): 8 Colbler Tooth
Rewards
Experience: 96525 EXP
Lant: 6,300 Lant Lant

1. Subjugate Excutive Colblers, and collect their teeth as proof of their deaths.

Note: when you walk over the bridge to the stone pit, at the right side of the pit is a path that has (a good way down the path) a single Colbler spawning and nothing else anywhere near. And while it doesn't look like it, with a bit of trial and error you can get out again on the right hand side of the top end of the path. Or continue towards Ghost Tree where you'll find some along with other scroll quest monsters.
