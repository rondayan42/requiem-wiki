South Hammerine Wood

From Requiem Wiki

Background

South Hammerine Wood is named after Hammerine, the ancient tree of life. In turn, the ancient tree Hammerine received its name from the old wise man that blessed it with eternal life. When Thanatos went berserk, the South Hammerine Wood suffered serious damage, especially since it was so close to Zudekha. The resulting ion explosion caused by Thanatos mutated the ancient Hammerine tree into a fearsome, hideous monster.
