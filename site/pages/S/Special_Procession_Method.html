Special Procession Method
Requirements
Level: 39
Rewards
Experience: 49230 EXP
Lant: 4800 Lant

Gather 8 Saurus Hide from Young Sauruses

Dialogue:

Saurus Hide is very difficult to process due to its extreme toughness. Fortunately for me, I've recently learned an expetional leather tanning method, so I'd like to try constructing high quality leather armor with Saurus Hide.Please gather Saurus Hide from Young Sauruses roaming around the Road of the Dead. You won't be able to skin Mother Sauruses since their skin grows tougher as they age, so make sure that you only hunt Young Sauruses.
