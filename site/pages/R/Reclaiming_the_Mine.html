Reclaiming the Mine

Part 1

Talk to Raisa in the Official Residence in Zakate. You will be told to talk to mine supervisor Jurgen in the Ioxenic headquarters.

  • Reward: 30 Lant / 11175 Exp

Part 2

Jurgen will tell you to talk to Arginte in the Prison mine. The mine is northeast of Zakate village. Arginte is just inside the mine entrance, by the resurrection point.

  • Reward: 40 Lant / 14130 Exp

Part 3

Deeper in the mine at 1045N 336S is the Cannibal monster which you need to kill three times. Best way is to camp for him, even though he will spawn in multiple places

  • Reward: 310 Lant/ 18495 Exp

Part 4

You need to acquire 8 Blackhand miners, and 8 Blackhand Supervisors. Then talk to Arginte at the beginning of the mine. Easier way to do it, camp at the entrance but make sure they don't mob you.

Part 5

You need to acquire 10 Mineral Lumps, by activating the Mineral Boxes throughout the mine.
