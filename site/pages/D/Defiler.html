From Requiem Wiki

Defiler
Job Base: Soul Hunter
Job Type: Advanced
Level Requirement: 50
Number of Skills: 3
Total Skill Points: Unknown

Characteristics

Defilers can control both the bodies and minds of their enemies. They use phantoms in conjunction with psychological exploitation to assume a great deal of control over their foes. Defilers can also absorb an enemy's HP to restore their own. Defilers can disrupt and attack their enemies with their de-buff abilities and wide range of DOT attacks. They can confuse and weaken multiple enemies at once.

Equipment Mastery

Defilers have access to the following equipment masteries:

  • Staff, Levels 5 to 6.
  • Wand, Levels 5 to 6.
  • Scythe, Levels 5 to 6.
  • Magic Shield, Levels 5 to 6.
  • Cloth Armor, Levels 5 to 6.
  • Leather Armor, Level 5.
