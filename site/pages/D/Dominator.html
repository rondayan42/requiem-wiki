Description

Dominators can assert control over the dead: they can restore dead creatures to life, or summon netherworldly beings to attack their enemies. They are also very skilled in curse magic.

Dominators generally summon monsters, and then cast buff magic to strengthen their minions. These minions are sent to the front lines of battle, and Dominators support them from a safe distance by de-buffing their enemies.

Make good use of your Summoned Creatures.
