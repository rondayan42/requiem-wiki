Numaren

From Requiem Wiki

Background

New Numaren-Map:Numarenreg.
Numaren is the region located on what used to be the continent's east peninsula. When Thanatos went berserk, the land suffered from tumultous calamities: parts of Numaren sunk into the ocean, resulting in an island separate from the main continent. The lost parts of Numaren were found by the scholars of the Hall of Recognition, but the Numaren island is now under the control of the Ioxenic because of certain events. There is a Ernil's tower which is said to be build by genius scientist Ernil.
