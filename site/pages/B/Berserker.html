Berserker
Job Base: Warrior
Job Type: Advanced
Level Requirement: 50
Number of Skills: 1
Total Skill Points: Unknown


Description

Berserkers have greater strength than Warriors, and can enhance their attacks by harnessing their burning rage. A party of Berserkers is always considered a considerable threat.

Berserkers can inflict critical damage with Two-Handed Weapons fueled by their rage. However, this critical attack change decreases if the rage isn't properly controlled.
