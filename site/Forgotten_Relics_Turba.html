Forgotten Relics(Turba)

From Requiem Wiki

  • This is the Quest Chain which ultimately gives you access to your two Posession Beasts - A unique gameplay element to Requiem.
  • This Quest chain can be obtained from Mayor Allectis in Zakate on Turba Island, and he is in the "Official Residences"
  • The following guide is for Turba island only


Forgotten Relics (1)

Speak with Ziolan, the Drunken Old Man in the Tavern

  • Reward: 100 Lant / 8850 Exp
