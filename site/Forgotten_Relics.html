Deliver the Old Relic to Scholar Peros in North Bivouac

Collect Red Fairy Ores(8) from Sprigans in North Iness Plains

Forgotten Relics(5)

Forgotten Relics(7)

Found in Nova Lux south of the Lux Plains wingrider, on road up hill.
